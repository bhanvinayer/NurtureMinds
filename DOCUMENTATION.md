tools\n- **Limited accessibility** in rural and underserved communities\n- **Fragmented solutions** requiring multiple expensive platforms\n\n### Solution\nA comprehensive web platform providing:\n- systems\n- AR/VR immersive experiences\n- Voice-controlled accessibility\n\n### Target Users\n- **Parents & Caregivers** - Primary users managing their children's development\n- **Children (Ages **Healthcare Providers** - Pediatricians and developmental specialists\n\n---\n\n## 🏗️ Architecture\n\n### System Overview\n\n```\n┌─────────────────┐    ┌─────────────────┐    ┌─────────────────┐\n│
